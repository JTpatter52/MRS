Q:
 How do I resize the FAQ window?

A:
 You can make this pop-up larger or smaller depending on your requirements. Open the "pop-closeup.js" in Notepad or a simple text editor. Then look for these variables: "var FAQ_width = 400" and "var FAQ_height = 500". You can edit these to any size you like but it's best not to go larger than 750 wide and 550 high.

The page you are looking at is called faq-2.htm

About Us:

MRS Appraisals is locally owned in Iowa City and was established in 1993. It was founded upon principles of honest, accurate, real estate analysis and reporting, in a timely manner through quality appraiser retention. Our appraisers have from 8 to 18 years of experience, providing an aggregate total of 89 years. This vast knowledge allows MRS Appraisals to provide accurate financial products suitable for in-house or secondary market clients.

Over the years MRS Appraisals has continued to keep pace with the latest current technology and is fully electronic “capable.” Our appraisers are FHA certified and all credentials may be found on our “Staff” page. Please feel welcomed to browse our site, call or email us for any specific information, requests or unique coverage area.

Not only are we long time citizens of the local area, we are proud sponsors of the Home Builders Association, Iowa City Chamber of Commerce, Kiwanis, Circle-K, Habitat for Humanity and we proudly support our local high school scholars and student athletes.

A full client list and references are available upon request.

MRS actively operates in Johnson, Iowa, Washington, Muscatine, and Cedar counties.
